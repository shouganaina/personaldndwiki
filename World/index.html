Magic and Its Boundaries

Magic in Morn is not equally accessible.

  • Divine and pact magic (clerics, paladins, warlocks) depends on bonds with gods, patrons, or primal forces.
  • Primal magic (druids) is tied to ancient nature spirits and elemental guardians.
  • True magic (wizards, sorcerers) is the most feared. Wizards weave veridium energy, turning study into power. Sorcerers are rarer still, born with inherent power often tied to bloodlines or cataclysmic events.
